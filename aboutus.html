Since 2001, the fertilizer market has seen a steep rise in productivity and equivalent pricing. In a tough competitive fight it is sometimes very difficult for companies to maintain the standard of products that it has already set before its customers. Jagrit Chemicals and Fertilizers, for the past 15 years, has been a dominant competitor in this field where it has never compromised with the quality and standard of its product.

JCF has proved to be the most trusted brand among its users for over 2 decades now. One thing if any, that may have scripted the success story of JCF is the fact that it has never compromised on its quality standards. Certified products which are verified by ace researchers make the very core of JCF's business.

JCF greatly values the trust that its customers has bestowed on it and aspires to live up to their expectations with no compromise on the quality of its product. JCF offers the most competitive rates for organic and inorganic fertilizers, micro-nutrients and chemicals that makes it the most preferred choice of customers.

After completing his chartered accountancy, Mr. Mukesh Lodha, (FCA) was very passionate to set up his own business. The opportunity soon came his way in the shape of his family run business of fertilizers. However, not being convinced with the conventional methodologies, Mr. Lodha soon founded his own concern. He strategized the manufacture of micro-nutrients from abroad and globalized the style of his orthodox family business into the best importer and supplier of organic and inorganic fertilizers and chemicals in Kolkata.
